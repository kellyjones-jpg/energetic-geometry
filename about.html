About This Project

Energetic Geometry is a web-based exploration of agrivoltaics through the lens of Suprematism, Op Art, and generative design. This project transforms data from the InSPIRE Agrivoltaics Map into dynamic, algorithmic visualizations that highlight the synergy between agriculture and solar energy.

Using p5.js and creative coding techniques, the site invites users to explore the visual energy of crops, pollinators, and land-use systems while foregrounding sustainability and accessibility. The visuals echo the modularity of stitched forms and the vibrancy of ecological interdependence.
